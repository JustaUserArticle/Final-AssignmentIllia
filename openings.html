Analysis of Popular Openings

The opening is the initial stage of the game, laying the foundation for all subsequent play in the middlegame.

Table 1: Overview of classic opening systems and their initial moves
Opening Type Opening Name Key Initial Moves Strategic Objective
Open Games Ruy Lopez (Spanish Opening) 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Bb5 Pressure on the c6 knight and the center
Italian Game 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Bc4 Attacking the weak f7 square
Semi-Open & Closed Games Sicilian Defense 1. e4 c5 Asymmetrical struggle for the center
Queen's Gambit 1. d4 d5 2. c4 Sacrificing a flank pawn to secure the center
