Classification and Value of Pieces

Each piece has its own unique character and movement path. Understanding the strength of your pieces is the foundation of a successful strategy.

Hierarchy of the Chess Army

  • 1. Major (Heavy) Pieces
    • Queen — The most powerful piece (valued at 9 pawns)
    • Rook — Moves in straight horizontal or vertical lines (valued at 5 pawns)
  • 2. Minor (Light) Pieces
    • Bishop — Operates along diagonal paths of its color (valued at 3 pawns)
    • Knight — The only piece capable of jumping over others (valued at 3 pawns)
