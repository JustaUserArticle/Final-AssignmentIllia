Classification and Value of Pieces

Each piece has its own unique movement path.

Hierarchy of the Chess Army

  • 1. Major (Heavy) Pieces
    • Queen - The most powerful piece (valued at 9)
    • Rook - Moves in straight horizontal or vertical lines (valued at 5)
  • 2. Minor (Light) Pieces
    • Bishop - Goes along diagonal paths of its color (valued at 3)
    • Knight — The only piece capable of jumping over others. Move in an "L" shape (valued at 3)
